Introduction

Welcome to our study on Stock Market Prediction. This study should take between 15 - 20 minutes to complete.

This study will ask 11 different exercises related to stock market prediction. For each question, you will be asked to determine if you believe a specified stock will increase or decrease in value after a certain period of time. From this study, we want to better understand what factors affect independent stock market predictions and how these predictions collected from multiple participants can be aggregated to produce accurate results.

Task

You will be assigned 11 exercises of 3 types:

  1. Color the Stock
    2. Here, you will be given 4 companies in each question along with some relevant information in the form of historical data for each company. You will need to select a color from the palette ranging from dark red to dark green to indicate whether you think the stock will go up or down. Remember, the darker the color, the more the jump/drop.

  2. Stock Projection
    3. In these types of questions, you will be given historical data for a particular company in the form of stock prices represented in a graph with movable points. Your task will be to move the last point of the graph to make a future estimate about the price of that stock for the required time period.

  3. Invest Money
    4. You will be given 2 questions of this type, each having a set of 5 companies. You will be given $10,000 to invest in these companies for a particular time period. You need to allocate this money to the different companies and use sliders to manipulate the pie chart reflecting this distribution.


NOTE: Your compensation will not be affected by your performance. Some questions are purposely challenging, and it may be difficult to answer the questions accurately. If you are unsure about a certain question, we ask that you please answer it to the best of your ability.
